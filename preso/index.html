Measuring energy consumption of BBOB fitness functions

Merelo-Guervós & Romero-López (UGR) García Valdez (Tec-Tijuana)

THE NYMPH: [...] We are stonecold and pure. We eat electric light.

James Joyce, Ulysses

In this paper, the author talks about how Ulysses is pretty much about electrification and how it flows and can be measured. It literally says that it has "intense attention ... to the ways in which the lived experience of individual bodies involves the disbursement of energy". In this case, the same happens with computers. James Joyce vive en Trieste entre 1905 y 1915; Ulysses was published in 1922; he wrote some chapters while living in Trieste, giving English classes to support his family.
